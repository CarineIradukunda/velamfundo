About Velamfundo

A creative learning space for discovery, innovation, collaboration and discourse

Velamfundo is a community learning center that creates learning opportunities for people in remote and low income areas. It is located in a Zimbabwean village called Mbembesi, largely populated by the Xhosa people. Its birth is largely influenced by the founder’s life experiences. Read his story below:

"When I relocated to my home village in 2008 I experienced the struggles of learning in rural Africa first-hand. I had to walk a long distance to school. This, coupled with an extended list of household chores I now had (fetch water from the borehole, cut firewood etc.) greatly diminished my study time and worse, I did not have any access to reading material at home. Since I was living with my grandmother I had to figure out problematic homework exercise on my own. Being a very determined learner I managed to endure all this and attained excellent results in my final primary school exams. As a result of the rarity of my excellent performance in rural schools, I got a scholarship to further my education at a prestigious high school. Ever since I graduated from high school I have never ceased learning. I’m a self-taught computer programmer and I am still learning more about computer science and other subjects that interest me. I rely on free university online courses to learn, chiefly the UC Berkeley EECS site, and free electronic books to further my knowledge and skills. I still live in Mbembesi village and this poses significant challenges to my work flow. I don’t have reliable internet access. When I go to the city I have to download course materials for all courses I intend to take. Also, I have to take unwanted breaks to go and charge my laptop at the nearest school or clinic. The fact that I’m living in a small village with no amenities bars me from accessing quality education. I founded Velamfundo to help learners with similar experiences and I also hope to benefit from the full implementation of the community learning center. I envision a future where rural students do not have to endure walking long distances, crossing flooded rivers and the grueling winter cold to get to school. A future where a child in the rural areas can just wake up and learn something new, make something new and change the world. That future is possible, and it starts with Velamfundo and you."

Velamfundo is a community of learners and educators all united by a common goal of making quality education more accessible to everyone. Members share the available resources to enhance their own and others’ learning. Velamfundo thrives on the culture of effective and continuous learning, innovation and sharing. At Velamfundo you will not only be inspired and challenged to grow intellectually, you will also get an opportunity to collaborate with other members on impactful and meaningful projects. Velamfundo is free and open for anyone.

The opportunity to change the future of rural education is in our hands

The world is changing at unprecedented rates and its problems are becoming more complex and the solutions to them more sophisticated. We are indeed in the Information Age. This age demands that we have more information experts and less common workers. At the heart of grooming experts that will take us into the future is a sound education that is accessible to all. Remote areas like rural areas are in the shadow region of this global development and indeed the education that could help them to develop faster. By focusing on delivering quality education opportunities in these areas we prepare for a future where the globe is developing at more homogenous rates.
