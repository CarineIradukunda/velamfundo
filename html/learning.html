Velamfundo Learning Center

Promoting a culture of intellectual self-determination and love for learning

Our center is equipped with a library where members can access reading material. We collaborate with local schools to design after school programs that will help students to attain academic excellence and inspire them to fall in love with learning. We also offer Reading & Writing classes to lower the high illiteracy rate in our community and partner with other educators and industry professionals to teach professional skills to equip our youth with easily transferrable and marketable skills. We aim to deliver a robust and high quality education that will take our community to the future.
